Simonson Cattle Company




The Homestead: Plentywood, MT

The homestead was founded in the early 1900's by a Norwegian immigrant who came to the United States in hopes of a new start. While farming the land, she met a cowboy who had been living on the open-range hunting wild cattle and breaking horses. This cowboy had been living on the land since the early 1800's when he came to the area as a young man with only his bedroll, saddle and saddle horse. This cowboy was William Simonson, the patriarch of the Simonson family. Throughout the years, the homestead has changed and evolved over time with one of the major changes being the establishment of the Simonson Cattle Company by owners Eric and Ken Simonson. Currently, the first of the fourth-generation is back to take on the farming and ranching operation. The first of this new generation is Marshall Simonson, the eldest son.
